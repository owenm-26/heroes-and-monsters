LEGENDS OF VALOR – HOW TO PLAY

Legends of Valor is a turn-based, MOBA-style game played on an 8x8 grid. You control a team of 3 heroes and battle waves of monsters. Your goal is to reach the monsters’ Nexus before any monster reaches yours.

GAME OBJECTIVE

• Heroes win if ANY hero reaches the Monsters’ Nexus (top of the board).
• Heroes lose if ANY monster reaches the Heroes’ Nexus (bottom of the board).

THE BOARD

• The game board is an 8x8 grid divided into 3 lanes:

Top Lane (columns 1–2)

Mid Lane (columns 4–5)

Bottom Lane (columns 7–8)
• Columns 3 and 6 are walls and cannot be entered.
• The top row is the Monsters’ Nexus.
• The bottom row is the Heroes’ Nexus.
• Each space may contain at most:

One hero

One monster

SPACE TYPES

• Nexus:

Heroes and monsters spawn here.

Heroes’ Nexus is also a market.

Reaching the enemy Nexus ends the game.

• Inaccessible:

Cannot be entered by heroes or monsters.

• Obstacle:

Cannot be entered.

A hero may remove it using a turn, turning it into a Plain.

• Plain:

No special effects.

• Bush:

Increases hero dexterity while inside.

Bonus is lost when leaving.

• Cave:

Increases hero agility while inside.

Bonus is lost when leaving.

• Koulou:

Increases hero strength while inside.

Bonus is lost when leaving.

Special spaces are randomly distributed but every board contains all types.

GAME FLOW

• The game is played in rounds.
• Each round has:

Heroes’ turn

Monsters’ turn

• At the start:

Player selects 3 heroes and assigns each to a lane.

Heroes spawn in their own Nexus spaces.

One monster spawns in each lane at the Monsters’ Nexus.

HERO ACTIONS (ONE PER HERO PER TURN)

Heroes may take ONE of the following actions:

• Change Weapon or Armor
• Use a Potion
• Attack
• Cast a Spell
• Move
• Teleport
• Recall
• (Optional) Pass Turn

Buying and selling items does NOT count as an action.

COMBAT RULES

• Attacks and spells may only target enemies in:

The current space

Adjacent spaces (north, south, east, west)
• A hero or monster may only attack ONE enemy per turn.
• Damage, armor, spells, gold, and experience work the same as in Monsters and Heroes.

MOVEMENT RULES

• Movement is only north, south, east, or west (no diagonals).
• A hero cannot:

Move into a space occupied by another hero

Move behind a monster in the same lane unless it is killed
• Monsters follow the same movement restrictions.
• Heroes can block monsters by standing in their path.

SPECIAL ACTIONS

• Teleport:

Move to a space adjacent to another hero in a DIFFERENT lane.

Cannot teleport:

Within the same lane

Onto an occupied space

Ahead of the target hero

Behind a monster

• Recall:

Instantly return to the hero’s original Nexus space.

Works from anywhere on the board.

END OF ROUND EFFECTS

• At the end of each round:

All living heroes recover 10% HP and 10% mana.
• If a hero dies:

They respawn at their Nexus at the start of the next round.

MONSTER SPAWNING
Every 10 rounds new monsters spawn in their nexus in each lane if there is room for them. All monsters are scaled to the level of heroes.
